Exercise 2.

Consider \(M_{16}\text{.}\)
What do expect the language of this machine to be? Rip \(D\text{,}\) then \(C\text{,}\) then \(B\text{,}\) and then \(A\text{.}\)
Answer.
\(L(M_{16})=\mbox{exactly three 1s with any amount of 0s or any amount of 0s}\)
Solution.
  1. Start.
  2. D: 2 transitions in, 1 transition out, \(2*1 = 2\text{,}\) so 2 new arrows
  3. C: 1 transition in, 1 transition out, \(1*1 = 1\text{,}\) so 1 new arrow
  4. B: 1 transition in, 1 transition out, \(1*1 = 2\text{,}\) so 1 new addition
  5. A: 1 transition in, 1 transition out, \(1*1 = 2\text{,}\) so 1 new arrow

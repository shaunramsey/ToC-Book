Before Turing Machines, there were a few different automatas that existed that laid the ground work for Turing’s thesis, but they had differing amounts of power and capabilities.
Linear Bounded Automata (LBAs)
A class of context seensitive languages, more powerful than CFLs, but nto as powerful as a TM
Muti-Tape
Same power as a TM
2-Stack PDA
Same power as a TM
Transducer
A TM who’s job is to modify the tape
Non-Determinism
Same power as a TM
Printer TM
Ennumerates/ prints all strings in its language

Exploration 6.

What strings can be made from \((aa+b)^*\text{?}\)
Some strings are:
  • \(\displaystyle aabbaa\)
  • \(\displaystyle (aa)^n\)
  • \(\displaystyle b^n\)
  • \(\displaystyle baabaa\)
  • \(\displaystyle aabbaaaa\)
A wide array of strings can be made from a simple expression declaration.

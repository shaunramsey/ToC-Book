Exercise 2.

Write a CFG for \(L_2 = (1^*01^*01^*)^*\)
Answer.
\(CFG_2:\)
\(S_2 \rightarrow T0T0T0TS_2 | \epsilon\)
\(T \rightarrow 1T | \epsilon\)
OR
\(CFG_2:\)
\(S_2 \rightarrow S_2S_2 | T0T0T | \epsilon\)
\(T \rightarrow 1T | \epsilon\)
OR
\(CFG_2:\)
\(S_2 \rightarrow U_30U_30U_3S_2 | \epsilon\)
\(U_3 \rightarrow 1U_3 | \epsilon\)
Both grammars can represent \(L_2\text{.}\)

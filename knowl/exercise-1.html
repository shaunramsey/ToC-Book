Exercise 1.

What is the language of \(M_3\text{?}\)
Hint.
Come up with ten strings in the language of \(M_3\text{.}\)
Come up with ten strings NOT in the language of \(M_3\text{.}\)
Do you see any patterns?
Answer.
\begin{equation*} L(M_3) = \mbox{the empty string and all binary strings divisible by 3} \end{equation*}
Solution.
It is helpful to look at patterns beyond those that are obvious and to consider ones that require a certain level of interpretation. Consider the test string lists from the hint, but this time the decimal number is included.

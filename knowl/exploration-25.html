We will walk through how to create a PDA from a CFL. Consider \(L_1\) where
\begin{equation*} L_1 = 0^n1^n \end{equation*}
The idea here is to push \(0\)s onto the stack until there are no more \(0\)s. We will then pop one \(0\) off of the stack for each \(1\) we read.

  2. \(10\epsilon\) - Read first 1, pop top 0 off of stack, do not push anything onto stack
  3. \(10\epsilon\) - Read rest of the 1s, pop top 0 off of stack, do not push anything onto stack
  4. \(\Delta\Delta\epsilon\) - Read in a \(\Delta\text{,}\) pop that \(\Delta\) off of stack, do not push anything onto stack

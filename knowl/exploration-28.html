Exploration 9.

Consider the CFG below. It is \(CFG_1\) in Chompsky Normal Form.
\(CFG_{1- CNF}\)
\(S_0 \rightarrow SS | AS | a | \epsilon\)
\(S \rightarrow SS | AS | a\)
\(A \rightarrow a\)
Considering \(CFG_{1 - CNF}\text{,}\) how many steps are there to produce "a"?
Answer 1.
1.
\begin{equation*} S_0 \rightarrow a \end{equation*}
How many steps are there to produce "aa"?
Answer 2.
3.
\begin{equation*} S_0 \rightarrow SS \rightarrow aS \rightarrow aa \end{equation*}
OR
\begin{equation*} S_0 \rightarrow AS \rightarrow aS \rightarrow aa \end{equation*}

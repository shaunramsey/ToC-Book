Exercise 3.

Create a power set for a FA with the following states: \(A\text{,}\) \(B\text{,}\) \(C\text{,}\) and \(D\text{.}\)
Answer.
\begin{align*} \{ \amp \emptyset, \\ \amp \{A\}, \{B\}, \{C\}, \{D\}, \\ \amp \{A,B\}, \{A,C\}, \{A,D\}, \{B,C\}, \{B,D\}, \{C,D\}, \\ \amp \{A,B,C\}, \{A,B,D\}, \{A,C,D\}, \{B,C,D\}, \\ \amp \{A,B,C,D\} \} \end{align*}

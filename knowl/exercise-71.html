Exercise 1.

Write a CFG for \(L_1 = (1+01)^*\)
Hint.
Consider the OR first. Then consider how to generate the reptition. There are multiple options for a grammar of this language.
Answer.
\(CFG_1:\)
\(S \rightarrow SS | 1 |01 | \epsilon\)
OR
\(CFG_1:\)
\(S \rightarrow 1S | 01S | \epsilon\)
OR
\(CFG_1:\)
\(S_1 \rightarrow U_1S_1 | U_2S_1 | \epsilon\)
\(U_1 \rightarrow 1\)
\(U_2 \rightarrow 01\)
Are all grammars that can represent \(L_1\text{.}\)

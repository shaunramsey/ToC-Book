Exploration 7.

Now, try to create your own PDA for the following language:
\begin{equation*} L_2 = \{a^i(bc)^i | i \geq 0 \} \end{equation*}
It is easiest to first write out the necessary steps and then draw the PDA diagram.
Here are the necessary steps:
  1. \(a\epsilon x\) - Read \(a\)s, do not pop anything from stack, push a \(x\) to the stack
  2. \(b\epsilon \epsilon\) - Read first \(b\text{,}\) do not pop anything from stack, do not push anything onto stack
  3. \(cx\epsilon\) - Read first \(c\text{,}\) pop first \(x\) from stack, do not push anything onto stack
  4. \(\Delta\Delta\epsilon\) - Read in a \(\Delta\text{,}\) pop that \(\Delta\) off of stack, do not push anything onto stack
It is important to note that we must loop through checking for both the \(b\) and \(c\text{.}\)

Exercise 2.

Identify patterns of state repetition from a written language.
\begin{equation*} L_2 = \{\mbox{any string with an odd number of 0s, starts with 1, ends with 111, and has at least two 0s}\} \end{equation*}
Hint.
Some test strings inlcude:
  • \(\displaystyle s^(0) = 1000111\)
  • \(\displaystyle s^(1) = 10100111\)
  • \(\displaystyle s^(2) = 1000000011111\)
Answer.
Character repetitions can exist in these two places:
  • \(\displaystyle 10(00)^*111\)
  • \(\displaystyle 10(1)^*00111\)
They can also be combined.

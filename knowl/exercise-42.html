Exercise 3.

Consider \(M_{17}\text{.}\)
What do expect the language of this machine to be? Rip \(B\text{,}\) then \(C\text{,}\) then \(A\text{,}\) and then \(D\text{.}\)
Answer.
\(L(M_{17})=\mbox{all strings with an odd number of 0s and an odd number of 1s}\)
Solution.
  1. Start.
  2. B: 2 transitions in and 2 transitions out, \(2*2=4\text{,}\) so 4 new arrows
  3. C: 2 transitions in and 2 transitions out, \(2*2=4\text{,}\) so 2 new \(+\)
  4. A: 2 transitions in and 1 transition out, \(2*1=2\text{,}\) so 2 new arrows

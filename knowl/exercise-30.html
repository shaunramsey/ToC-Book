Exercise 4.

What is the language of this Regular Expression?
\begin{equation*} REX_4 = (\_+a+b+c+...+Y+Z)(\_+a+b+c+...+Y+Z+0+1+...+8+9)^* \end{equation*}
Your answer does not need to be in mathematical notation.
Hint.
Some example strings inlcude:
  • \(\displaystyle al\)
  • \(\displaystyle abba\_1\)
  • \(\displaystyle \_012\)
  • \(\displaystyle int\)
Answer.
\(L(REX_4)=\mbox{underscore or any alphabetic character followed by any number of underscores or any alphanumeric characters}\)
